Steven Gerrard

  • Liverpool
  • La Galaxy
  • Rangers
  • Aston Villa
  • 1998 - Debuts For Liverpool against Blackburn Rovers.
  • 2001 - Wins the treble: FA Cup, League Cup, and UEFA Cup.
  • 2003 - Wins the league cup again with Liverpool.
  • 2005 - The year that lives forever. Gerrard's dominant performance to bring home the Champions League trophy to Anfield.
  • 2006 - Another year, another trophy. This time sealing the F.A Cup with a win against West Ham with a last minute, thirty-yard half-volley. A match that would come to be known as 'The Steven Gerrard final'.
  • 2007 - One more Champions League final, against AC Milan like in 2005. This time Milan get their revenge.
  • 2009 - Wins a personal accolade: Footballer of the Year. Arguably deserved it on many more occasions.
  • 2012 - Steven's last trophy with Liverpool as a player, the League Cup, under the management of fellow Liverpool legend, Kenny Dalglish.
  • 2015 - After a long and successful career at Liverpool, Gerrard moves to LA Galaxy in the USA.
  • 2016 - The curtains close on Steven Gerrard's playing career. Loved by millions, and one of the best players of the Premier League era.
  • 2017 - Gerrard begins the management phase of his life, starting with Liverpool u18s.
  • 2018 - Leaving Liverpool once again, Steven's management career gains traction as he joins one of the biggest teams in Scotland as manager, Rangers FC.
  • 2021 - After guiding Rangers to their first SPL title win in 10 years, Gerrard moves back to England. Wins his first game as a manager in the Premier League, with Aston Villa.
